About the Game

Magical Dash is a fast-paced infinite runner where players survive a spooky adventure! With the ability to magically transform between a witch and a cat, players must dash through an enchanted world, collecting gems while dodging haunted obstacles like pumpkins, bats, and ghosts. Master the transformations and test your reflexes in this race through the night!

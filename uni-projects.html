University Projects

Aurora

Sole Project

This project was created as part of my Master's degree. It was titled Incorporating Environmental Storytelling to Increase Immersion in 3D Game Worlds. The main objective of this project regards incorporating environmental storytelling elements into game worlds to test the effect this can have on immersion. These were both narrative environmental storytelling elements and affordance elements to help guide the player. It featured puzzles to be solved, a variety of unique areas to explore and a narrative to fit the context of the environmental storytelling.

Jambulance

Team Project

Project created as part of my final year project at university. Titled Jambulance I acted as the lead designer and artist as part of a team to create a rhythmic driving game. Midway into the development I became the producer. Influenced by the 2017 film Baby Driver by Edgar Wright, the entire game is syced to the background music and requires player to time their movement to avoid obstacles.

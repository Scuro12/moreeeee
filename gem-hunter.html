About the Game

Gem Hunter 3D is a thrilling 2.5D platformer that takes players deep into a mysterious cave filled with hidden treasures. As you explore, you'll uncover precious gems, each containing a challenging question. Answer correctly to boost your score, which can be used to upgrade equipment and unlock powerful abilities. Enhance your gear to discover even more hidden gems and areas to conquer the depths of this underground adventure!

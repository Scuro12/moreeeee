About Me

A passionate Game Designer and Developer with a strong focus on crafting immersive and engaging gaming experiences. Currently based in Reading, UK, I work at Twinkl as a Game Designer/Developer, where I design and develop innovative educational games.

Experience & Expertise

With a background in creative technologies and computer games technology, I have extensive experience in game design, level design, and narrative integration. My expertise spans:

  • Game Development: Proficient in Unity, C#, Unreal Engine, and HLSL.
  • Environmental Storytelling: Crafting worlds that enhance player immersion.
  • Prototyping & Iteration: Rapid testing and refining of game mechanics.
  • Educational Game Design: Creating engaging learning experiences.

Professional Journey

As a Game Designer/Developer at Twinkl, I have:

  • Designed and developed 10+ published games.
  • Collaborated in interdisciplinary teams to create interactive experiences.
  • Optimised workflows for efficient game development.

Education

  • MSc Creative Technologies, University of Portsmouth (2020-2021): Specialised in environmental storytelling and player immersion using Unreal Engine.
  • BSc (Hons) Computer Games Technology, University of Portsmouth (2017-2020): Strong Focus on player psychology and game design principles.

Interests

Beyond game development, I co-founded a thriving gaming tournament series, fostering a 500% community growth over ten years. I also enjoy exploring new trends in game design, and engaging in personal projects, including material and texture design with a library of 200+ assets.

About the Game

Packet Switching is a strategic networking game where players navigate digital pathways to find the shortest or longest route from one PC to another. Click on routers to move through the network, simulating how data packets travel across the internet. Think ahead, choose the most efficient or challenging path, and master the fundamentals of networking in this engaging and educational experience!

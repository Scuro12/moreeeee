About the Game

Raccoon Racer is a fast-paced 2D arcade racing game where quick thinking leads to victory! Players must choose the correct on-screen pop-ups to gain speed boosts and outpace their opponents. Designed to teach online safety, the game challenges players to identify safe and unsafe digital interactions while racing through a vibrant, animated world powered by a custom shader-driven moving background.

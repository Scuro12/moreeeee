About the Game

Monkey Mischief is a thoughtful math adventure where players solve multiplication challenges to help their monkey climb the right vine. Each question is based on the 3 times table, and players must choose the vine with the correct answer to climb higher and collect bananas. Pick wisely, test your multiplication skills, and climb your way to success in this engaging learning experience!
